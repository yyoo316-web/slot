Privacy Policy — Golden Dragon Slot

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Golden Dragon Slot ("the App") is a free entertainment game. This policy explains what data the App handles. In short: we do not collect any data.

1. Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. There are no user accounts, no sign-ups, no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party tracking of any kind.

2. Local Data

Game state (such as your virtual coin balance) exists only in your device's memory while the App is open and is not sent anywhere.

3. Internet Access

The App loads its game content and display fonts from the web (GitHub Pages and Google Fonts). These requests do not include any personal information beyond what any standard web request contains.

4. No Real Money

The App is for entertainment only. It does not involve real money, in-app purchases, gambling, or prizes of any real-world value. Virtual coins have no monetary value and cannot be purchased, sold, or redeemed.

5. Children

Because the App contains simulated gambling imagery, it is rated for adult audiences in accordance with app store content rating policies.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please open an issue at github.com/yyoo316-web/slot.
